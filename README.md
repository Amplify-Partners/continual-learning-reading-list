# Memory Augmented Neural Networks Reading List

A reading list of relevant papers and projects on memory augmented neural networks for retrieval and continual learning.

## Foundational Memory Augmented Architectures

- [Memory Networks](https://arxiv.org/abs/1410.3916) — Weston et al. (Oct 2014)
- [Neural Turing Machines](https://arxiv.org/abs/1410.5401) — Graves et al. (Oct 2014)
- [Differentiable Neural Computers](https://deepmind.google/blog/differentiable-neural-computers/) — Graves et al. (Oct 2016)
- [Scaling Memory Augmented Neural Networks with Sparse Reads and Writes](https://arxiv.org/abs/1610.09027) — Rae et al. (Oct 2016)
- [Meta-Learning with Memory-Augmented Neural Networks](https://proceedings.mlr.press/v48/santoro16.pdf) — Santoro et al. (Jun 2016)

## Retrieval

- [Retrieval-Augmented Generation for Knowledge-Intensive NLP Tasks](https://www.patricklewis.io/publication/rag/) — Lewis et al. (May 2020)
- [RETRO: Improving Language Models by Retrieving from Trillions of Tokens](https://arxiv.org/abs/2112.04426) — Borgeaud et al. (Dec 2021)

## Memory Layers and Product Keys

- [Large Memory Layers with Product Keys](https://arxiv.org/abs/1907.05242) — Lample et al. (Jul 2019)
- [Memory Layers at Scale](https://arxiv.org/abs/2412.09764) — Berges et al. (Dec 2024)
- [Conditional Memory via Scalable Lookup: A New Axis of Sparsity for Large Language Models](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.07372) — Cheng et al. (Jan 2026)

## Context Compaction and Meta-Harnesses

- [Cartridges: Lightweight and General-Purpose Long Context Representations](https://arxiv.org/abs/2506.06266) — Eyuboglu et al. (Jun 2025)
- [Fast KV Compaction via Attention Matching](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.16284) — Zweiger et al. (Feb 2026)
- [Meta-Harnesses](https://yoonholee.com/meta-harness/) — Lee et al. (2026)

## Test-Time Training and Continual Learning

- [Continual Learning via Sparse Memory Fine-Tuning](https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.15103) — Lin et al. (Oct 2025)
- [End to End Test-Time Training](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.23675) — Tandon et al. (Dec 2025)
- [Fast-weight Product Key Memory](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.00671) — Zhao et al. (Jan 2026)
- [Learning to Discover at Test Time](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.16175) — Yuksekgonul et al. (Jan 2026)
- [Titans: Learning to Memorize at Test Time](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2501.00663) – Behrouz et al. (Dec 2024)
